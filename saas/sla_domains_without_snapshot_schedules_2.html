Continuous Data Protection-only SLA Domains for Amazon RDS

RSC workloads, such as Amazon RDS, support both continuous data backup and scheduled backups. You can enable them as needed. With Continuous Data Protection-only SLA Domains, you can exclude snapshot schedules for supported workloads by turning off the frequency and retention when creating or editing the Continuous Data Protection-only SLA Domains.

Continuous Data Protection (CDP) is a data backup approach that continuously captures and protects data changes in real-time, allowing precise restoration to any specific point in time. This ensures robust protection against data loss and provides maximum data integrity.

Note:
CDP-only SLA Domains are supported only for Amazon RDS.

Benefits

CDP-only SLA Domains offer significant advantages by leveraging reduced costs associated with log backups compared to regular scheduled backups. Key benefits include:
  • Minimized resource load: By continuously backing up data, CDP reduces the demand on system performance, as it eliminates the need for frequent full or incremental scheduled backups.
  • Lower expenses: The cost savings from reduced log backups and simpler data protection processes translate to lower overall expenses.
  • Enhanced data integrity: Continuous backup ensures that data changes are captured immediately, reducing the risk of data loss and enhancing overall data integrity.

Limitations

The following are the certain limitations associated with CDP-only SLA Domains:
  • CDP-only SLA Domains do not support data archiving or replication.
  • CDP-only SLA Domains do not support taking on-demand snapshots.
