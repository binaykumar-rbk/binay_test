Relic EC2 instance or EBS volume

When an EC2 instance or EBS volume is deleted from AWS and is still available on RSC, that instance or volume is no longer managed or protected by any SLA Domain.

The EC2 instance or EBS volume becomes inactive. RSC performs periodic refresh jobs to update data to discover inactive EC2 instance or EBS volume.

For an inactive EC2 instance or EBS volume that has no snapshot, RSC archives that instance or volume. RSC manages inactive EC2 instances or EBS volumes that contain snapshots as relics. The remaining snapshots of relic objects are managed by the last SLA Domain policy that was applied to the object before deletion.
