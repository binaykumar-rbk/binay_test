Workload-level data management permissions

Some data management permissions can be assigned at several different hierarchy levels, including the entire workload level.

The data management permissions model enables activation of privileges at the subscription level, the workload level, or the individual object level. A user with a privilege granted at one level implicitly gains that privilege for objects of a lower hierarchy level contained by the higher-level workload. For example, a user with the Manage Protection privilege at the workload level for Exchange mailboxes can manage SLA Domains assigned to any Exchange mailbox on the RSC domain. A privilege granted at a specific object level does not extend upwards in the hierarchy.

The following table describes the privileges supporting this workload-level behavior.
Privilege Description
View Inventory Enables viewing of items of the specified workload level and any objects at lower hierarchy levels. For example, View Inventory at the workload level grants the ability to view individual objects.
Manage Protection Enables management of SLA Domains assigned to the specified workload level and any objects at lower hierarchy levels.
Take On-demand Snapshot Enables the taking of on-demand snapshots at the specified workload level and any objects at lower hierarchy levels.
Restore to Origin Enables recovery from a snapshot to the original source. These recovery operations overwrite the data at the source.
Export Files Enables recovery from a snapshot to a target other than the source.
