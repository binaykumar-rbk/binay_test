Adding claim rules for OneLogin

Add claim rules to tell OneLogin how to format the claims sent to RSC.

About this task

Specify values for the email and group claim attributes that are included in the claims sent to RSC.

Procedure

  1. From the left-side menu of the SAML Test Connector (Advanced) page, select Parameters.
  2. Click the plus icon to add a rule.
    The New Field dialog box appears.
  3. In Field Name, enter http://schemas.xmlsoap.org/ws/2005/05/identity/claims/emailaddress.
  4. Click Save.
    The Edit Field dialog box appears.
  5. In Value, select Email.
  6. Under Flags, select Include in SAML assertion and click Save.
    The new field appears as a custom parameter under the SAML Test Connector (Advanced) Field column.
  7. Click the plus icon to add the next rule.
  8. In Field Name, enter http://schemas.xmlsoap.org/claims/Group.
  9. Under Flags, select Include in SAML assertion and Multi-value parameter.
  10. Click Save.
  11. In Value, select User Roles and Semicolon Delimited input (Multi-value output).
    This maps the User Roles in OneLogin to the Group field in the SAML assertion.
  12. Under Flags, select Include in SAML assertion and click Save.
    The new field appears as a custom parameter under the SAML Test Connector (Advanced) Field column.
  13. Click Save to finish the configuration.

Results

OneLogin configures the Email and Group claim rules.

What to do next

Specify an access policy as described in Granting access to RSC for OneLogin users.
