Customer-controlled archival location versus RCV

A comparative overview of the customer-controlled archival location and Rubrik Cloud Vault.

When evaluating archiving solutions, you must consider the relative advantages and disadvantages of using a customer-controlled archival location versus Rubrik Cloud Vault (RCV). RCV, a hosted service administered by Rubrik, eliminates the necessity to provision and manage underlying cloud resources, thereby enabling you to concentrate on your primary business objectives.
The following table provides a comprehensive comparison of these two archival locations:
Aspect Customer-controlled archival location RCV
Control and ownership Full customer control and ownership. Managed by Rubrik, ownership shared.
Provisioning Customer ownership for configuring storage. Rubrik provides and manages cloud resources.
Provisioning effort High, due to customer ownership for provisioning and managing resources. Low, as Rubrik sets up necessary cloud accounts and resources.
Security Self-implemented and personalized security measures. Built-in security features and compliance standards.
Cost Variable, as it depends on the customer setup. License-based pricing, predictable costs.
