Protecting Microsoft Teams data

Specify a data protection policy for RSC to use when protecting Microsoft Teams data.

Procedure

  1. Log in to RSC.
  2. On the navigation bar, click Inventory.
    The Inventory page appears.
  3. On the Microsoft 365 inventory card, click View.
    The Microsoft 365 dashboard appears.
  4. Select a subscription and click Enter.
    The Microsoft 365 subscription page appears.
  5. Click Teams.
    The list of teams appears.
  6. Select a team.
    You can select multiple objects.
  7. Click Manage Protection.
    The Manage Protection page appears.
  8. Choose a protection option.
    OptionDescription
    SLA Domain The SLA Domain is assigned to the selected Teams objects.
    Clear existing assignment The SLA Domain is assigned from the next higher level object based on inheritance rules.
    Don't protect The selected Teams objects are excluded from all further SLA Domain assignments.
  9. Click Next.
  10. Review the information on the summary page and click Assign.

Results

RSC assigns the protection option to the selected Teams objects.
