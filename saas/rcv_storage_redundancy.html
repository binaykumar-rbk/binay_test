RCV storage redundancy

Rubrik Cloud Vault offers various storage redundancy options across different cloud platforms to meet your data availability and durability targets. These options provide protection against hardware failures, network outages, and natural disasters.

Storage redundancy is an important aspect of disaster recovery and cyber resiliency, ensuring that your data remains accessible and durable even during hardware failures, network or power outages, and natural disasters. Rubrik Cloud Vault (RCV) provides different storage redundancy options to provide multiple levels of data protection. For existing RCV locations hosted on Azure, you can change the redundancy option.

Note:
You can purchase RCV entitlements by the storage tier and redundancy option, and use them across all supported cloud platforms. For example, if you purchase a multi-zone entitlement for the RCV backup tier, you can use it for Azure, AWS, and GCP.

Storage redundancy options

There are multiple storage redundancy options that RCV supports:
  • Single zone: RSC stores your data redundantly across multiple devices within a single Availability Zone. It provides a cost-effective solution that protects against local hardware failures. Single zone ensures data durability within the same data center, protecting against server rack and drive failures.
  • Multi-zone: RSC stores your data on multiple devices across a minimum of three Availability Zones in a region. Multi-zone redundancy ensures higher data availability and durability, protecting against failures at the data center level by distributing data across multiple, physically separated locations within the same region. This redundancy option provides higher data availability and durability, reducing the risk of data loss due to localized failures, such as power outages, cooling system failures, or network issues specific to one data center.
  • Multi-region: RSC replicates your data to a secondary region, located hundreds of miles away from the primary region. Multi-region stores three copies of your data in the primary region and three additional copies in the secondary region, ensuring data availability and durability even during regional outages. This redundancy option provides an additional layer of protection against regional disasters, ensuring business continuity and data availability in recovery scenarios.

The Rubrik Cloud Vault storage tiers topic provides a comparison between the different storage tiers.
