Skipped items during Microsoft 365 recovery

RSC might skip recovering some Microsoft 365 items during the recovery process. To provide greater insight into the recovery process, RSC events provide detailed information about the items that RSC could not recover.

Microsoft 365 recovery process might fail, for example, because of an internal server error. RSC might also skip recovering certain items during the recovery process. After recovery, the correspondingRSC event displays the recovery status and details about any items skipped during recovery.

You can download a list of the skipped items during recovery in a CSV file, which provides details such as item name and path. You can also export the skipped items in a ZIP archive for OneDrive, Exchange mailboxes, and Teams files.

RSC events classify the recovery as successful, partially successful, or failed, based on the percentage of items skipped during recovery. You can view events on the Events tab.
  • For successful recoveries, the percentage of skipped items is 0%.
  • For partially successful recoveries, the percentage of skipped items is less than or equal to 5%.
  • For failed recoveries, the percentage of skipped items is more than 5%.
