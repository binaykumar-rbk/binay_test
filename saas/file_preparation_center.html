File preparation center

The File Preparation Center tab on the Events page provides information about reports downloaded or sent to email recipients.

The File Preparation Center lists reports downloaded or emailed to users in the past 24 hours.

When a report is downloaded or sent to email recipients from the Reports page, the name of the report appears in a tray at the bottom of the screen. Clicking the View all items button on the tray opens the File Preparation Center tab.

You can also use the download button located on the tray or in the File Preparation Center tab to download reports.
