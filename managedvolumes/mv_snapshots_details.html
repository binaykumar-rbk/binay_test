Snapshots details

Details of snapshots taken for SLA Managed Volumes and Managed Volumes.

The Snapshots tab displays the retention SLA Domains for all scheduled snapshots associated with relic, replicated relic, or unprotected data sources. The Snapshots tab also displays the combined number of on-demand snapshots and retrieved snapshots in a separate column. The Snapshots tab enables changes to the retention policy or deletion of a given snapshot.

While scripts on the data host determine the frequency of writing snapshots to the Managed Volume, SLA Domain policies determine retention, replication, and archiving for SLA Managed Volume.

You can recover and download snapshots that have expired on the local page, but available in archival locations.

This table describes the details available in the Snapshots tab.

Field Description
Snapshot Date and Time Date and time when the snapshot was taken.
Type
  • On demand displays snapshots created through the on-demand snapshot process.
  • Policy based displays snapshots created based on the policies and rules of an assigned SLA Domain.
  • Downloaded displays snapshots retrieved from an archival location.

    Retrieved snapshots and on-demand snapshots that are not assigned to an SLA Domain are included in both Relic and Unprotected listings.
Source Expiration Date Expiration date for the snapshot as determined by the SLA Domain.
Archival Location Archival location and the expiration date for the snapshot on that archival location.
Archival Expiration Date
Replication Target 1 First replication location and the expiration date for the snapshot on that replication location.
Replication Expiration Date
Replication Target 2 Second replication location and the expiration date for the snapshot on that replication location.
Replication Expiration Date
Related concepts
  • Legal hold for snapshot retention
    Legal hold ensures that snapshots are retained indefinitely until the hold is removed, overriding the scheduled expiry of an SLA Domain policy. This ensures that snapshots are available as long as necessary to meet legal obligations.
