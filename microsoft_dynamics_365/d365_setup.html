You can set up a Microsoft Dynamics 365 subscription in RSC and start protecting your Microsoft Dynamics 365 environments.

Setting up a Microsoft Dynamics 365 subscription in RSC to protect your Dynamics 365 environments involves detailed planning. This process includes:
  • Referring to the recommendations from Rubrik for adding a Microsoft Dynamics 365 subscription.
  • Learning about the required permissions.
After you complete the planning, you can proceed with adding your Microsoft Dynamics 365 subscription to RSC.
