Configuring SMB authentication for SMB shares

For NetApp and Isilon systems, configure SMB credentials manually at the SMB share level to enable SMB authentication and protection for SMB for all namespaces and shares on the system.

Procedure

  1. Log in to RSC.
  2. Click and select Data Protection.
    The Data Protection dashboard appears.
  3. On the navigation bar, click Inventory.
    The Inventory page appears.
  4. Click the NAS Systems inventory card.
    The NAS Systems page appears.
  5. Click Shares.
  6. Select the NAS SMB share.
    You can search for the NAS SMB share by name. You can also use the Protocol, System, and Namespace filters to filter for the NAS share.
  7. Click . Then click Edit Share.
  8. In SMB Username, enter a username.
  9. In SMB Password, enter a password.
  10. Click Update.

Results

RSC configures SMB authentication for the selected SMB share.
