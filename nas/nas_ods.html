On-demand snapshots of NAS shares and directories

In addition to policy-based snapshots, you can use the on-demand snapshot process to take snapshots of protected NAS shares and directories.

According to the rules of the associated SLA Domain, the Rubrik cluster automatically creates policy-based snapshots of protected NAS shares and directories. You can create additional snapshots of protected NAS shares and snapshots of unprotected NAS shares by using the on-demand snapshot process.

You can take an on-demand snapshot at the NAS share or directory level. You can view the number of on-demand snapshots for the object on the snapshots card on the NAS share details page.
