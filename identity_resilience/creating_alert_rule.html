Creating an alert rule

Create a custom alert rule to generate alerts for specific activities detected in an Identity Provider.

Procedure

  1. Log in to RSC.
  2. Click and select Identity Resilience.
    The Identity Resilience dashboard appears.
  3. On the navigation bar, click Activity > Alert Rules.
    The Alert Rules page appears.
  4. Click Create Alert Rule.
    The Create Alert Rule wizard appears.
  5. In Overview, enter the alert rule name and description.
  6. In Severity Level, select the severity level for the alert you want to create.
  7. Click Next.
    The Conditions page appears.
  8. In When, define the condition that, when met, triggers an alert.
    You can add multiple conditions while creating an alert rule using the Add Condition option on the Conditions page.
  9. Optional: Click Add Condition to define more conditions that, when met, triggers an alert.
    OptionDescription
    And When you select the relationship as And, RSC will trigger an alert only when all the configured conditions are met.
    Or When you select the relationship as Or, RSC will trigger an alert when either of the conditions is met.
    Note:
    Changing the relationship applies to all the additional conditions. Rubrik does not support a combination of And Or relationships for additional conditions.
  10. Click Next.
  11. Review your selections and click Create.

Results

You have created a custom alert rule. RSC will now trigger alerts for future activities in your Identity Provider when the configured conditions are met.
