Restoring an Amazon S3 bucket or its objects

When you want to recover an entire Amazon S3 bucket or recover individual objects to their source location, restore the S3 bucket or the individual objects by using the in-place recovery option.

Procedure

  1. Log in to RSC.
  2. Click and selectData Protection.
  3. On the navigation bar, click Inventory.
    The Inventory page appears.
  4. Click the AWS S3 inventory card.
    The AWS S3 page appears with the list of S3 buckets.
  5. To recover the objects associated with an S3 bucket or to recover the entire S3 bucket, select the S3 bucket that you want to recover.
    The page associated with the selected S3 bucket appears.
  6. Select a snapshot from the calendar view.
    The Snapshot details appear.
  7. Open the ellipsis menu for the snapshot and select Recover.
    The AWS S3 Recovery wizard appears.
  8. To recover individual files or the entire S3 bucket, choose from the following options, and click Next.
    OptionDescription
    Recover objects Recovers individual folders and files from the selected snapshot.
    Recover entire S3 bucket Recovers the existing S3 bucket from the selected snapshot.
  9. (Recover objects) Select the files you want to recover and click Next.
  10. To restore the bucket or its objects to its source location, select In-place recovery and then click Next.
    A note appears indicating that the In-place recovery replaces the specified objects from the snapshot you have selected.
    The S3 Bucket Name and Region details are displayed.
  11. Click Recover.

Results

RSC performs in-place recovery of the selected S3 bucket or the associated objects and restores the S3 bucket or its objects to its source location.
